Purpose

Create a site to be a portfolio for my work, as well as my writings. I am an aspiring writer and in future I hope to be published. My plan therefore is to post any book covers here in a convenient format for people to find out more about me.

Audience

Future readers and any prospective employers. I want to make it easy for said audience to find my work as well as see things that I can do.
